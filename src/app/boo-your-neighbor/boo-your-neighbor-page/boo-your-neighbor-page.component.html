So you've been boo'ed!

That is great! I hope you enjoyed your special treats. Let's keep the fun going. Here are the next steps:

  • Place your "We've Been Booed" sign on your front door or window. This is how you tell your neighbors not to boo you again - or don't if you want to be boo'ed again.
  • Preferably within two days, make two copies of the instructions and the "We've Been Boo'ed" sign, and gather two sets of special treats.
  • Deliver the treats and instructions to two other houses in Belle Creek who do not already have the "We've Been Booed" sign.

You can download a copy of the instructions or you can pick up pre-printed copies at the family center. You can also buy pre-built special treats for $5. The proceeds of which will be used for future events.
