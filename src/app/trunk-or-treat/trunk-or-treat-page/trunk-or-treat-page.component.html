Trunk-Or-Treat!

There will be a trunk-or-treat in the parking lot that will run from 5 to 7 pm. Candy will be provided for you, all you need to do is show up with a decorated car and hand out candy to the little gouls and goblins. There will be trophy for best decorated car. So craft yourself the most frightening car you can and show us what you can do.
